Pyin Oo Lwin

Pyin Oo Lwin or Pyin U Lwin, formerly and colloquially referred to as Maymyo, is a scenic hill town in the Mandalay Region, Myanmar, some 67 kilometers east of Mandalay, and at an elevation of 1,070 metres. The town was estimated to have a population of around 255,000 in 2014
